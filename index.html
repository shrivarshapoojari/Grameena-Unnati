About Us

Gramina Unnati is a society dedicated to providing professional support for projects in agriculture with special emphasis on organic cultivation, agri businesses and rural upliftment.We have Mr. Anil Kumar Shetty , Ms. Sunitha HR and Mr. R Kantaraj as three pillars of our society.We are building a bridge between farmers and corporates.We are working with Amazon , Big Basket like multinational companies to promote organic farming and empowering the rural community.Since past 30 years we have been working with about 10,000 farmers directly or through Government schemes.
